Pasta arrabiatta recepie

Description

One day, my girlfriend and I sat in front of the open fridge and wandered "What can we eat with a can of tomatoes, garlic and some pasta ?

Some say that "creativity comes from constraint" and that day we found out that it can't be more true

Stay hooked to learn this dangerously simple, but delicious recepie that can save you time and money

Things that you need

  • Tomatoes or a can of tomatoes
  • 7 garlic gloves (yes, you need alot of them)
  • Some olive oil
  • Pasta (any should do)
  • Fresh basel
  • Fresh parsley
  • Some cayenne powder or a hot pepper

Steps

  1. Add some olive oil to the pan and let it heat up
  2. Chop the garlic and add it to the pan
  3. Let it cook for a while until you see some browning of the garlic
  4. Add the tomatoes to the mix (be careful here because the water of the tomatoes doesn't mix well with the hot oil and it will scisel violently)
  5. Season and reduce the heat of the sauce to a gentle simmer
  6. Cook the pasta as shown on the packaging
  7. Serve and enjoy

The beauty of this recepie is that you can go in many ways to make it your own

If you crave some protein, you can add some lard, bacon or chiken breast. If you want it more creamy, add some cream and parmesian. The limit is your imagination

Don't forget that the secret ingredient is sharing so be sure to bring in your loved ones to enjoy it with you
