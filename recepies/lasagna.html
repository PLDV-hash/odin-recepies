Lasagna recepie

Description

A lasagna is a classic. I still remember as a child the big plate that my mom pulled out of the oven. If you want to give your loved ones these kind of good memories, stick with this simple recepie of a lasagna.

Things that you need

  • 500 grams of minced beef meat
  • One oignon
  • 5 gloves of garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basel
  • Fresh parsley
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Cook the meat in a hot searing pot
  2. Take out the cooked meat and add in the oignon
  3. Add the tomatoes and season
  4. Add back the cooked meat back and let it cook for an hour
  5. While it cooks, in a pot filled with boiling water, add the lasagna noodles and let them cook as shown on the package
  6. Put the ricotta cheese in a bowl and add in some seasoning
  7. After everything is done cooking, to a tray, add a bed of noodles and on top add a layer of the sauce
  8. Add another layer of noodels on top, and put a lyer of ricotta cheese on top
  9. Continue to build up your lasagna with alternate layers until you are done with the ingredients
  10. Cover your tray with aluminum paper and add put it in the oven to bake for 25 minutes at 200C or 375F

And voila, a perfect lasagna to enjoy with your loved ones !

Remember that the secret ingredient is sharing so be shure to gather everybody around to enjoy this delitious meal
