Chilli con carne recepie

Description

One day, when I was a student, I wanted something more than the same old instant noodles. So I scoured the internet for a recepie that was delicious, but didn't break the bank. And if it could last for a long time, it would be nice too. And what I found changed my cooking life forever !

This simple recepie checked all the boxes for me, and it's great for a begginner chef, as it's not complicated and goes over the basics elegently.

Things that you need

  • 500 grams of minced beef meat
  • One oignon
  • 5 gloves of garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basel
  • Fresh parsley
  • Rice
  • One can of red beans
  • Cumin
  • Dark chocolate (trust me)
  • Carrots
  • Bell pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the meat in a hot searing pot
  2. Take out the cooked meat and add in the oignon and the other vegetables
  3. Add the tomatoes and season
  4. Add back the cooked meat back and let it cook for an hour
  5. Rince the beans and add them in
  6. Let that cook for 1h-2h
  7. While it cooks, it's a good time to rince the rice and let it cook
  8. By the time that the sauce is done, add a couple of dark chocolate squares to add some body to the sauce
  9. Serve with the rice on the side, and optionally, some sour cream

And there you have it, a delicious bowl of Chilli con carne

Remember that the secret ingredient is sharing so be sure to gather everybody around to enjoy this delitious meal
